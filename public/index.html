◈ WEB3 SCHOOL

Learn Web3 by Doing It

Web3 education is mostly hype. We built this because every person who comes into crypto gets rekt in month one — not from DeFi complexity, but from not understanding wallet security, LP math, or how wallets actually work. Web3 School teaches what the scams are made of.

Most courses teach you how to use protocols. We teach you how they work under the hood, what can go wrong, and how to protect yourself. This is operational knowledge — not speculation. The kind of stuff that saves you from losing everything to a bad signature.

Whether you're a dev looking to understand on-chain mechanics or an operator building sovereign infrastructure, this is the foundation. We don't do roadmaps. We ship first.
