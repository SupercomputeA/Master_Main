NewsDesk is live — decentralized journalism infrastructure for the on-chain era.

## What is NewsDesk?

NewsDesk is the content arm of Supercompute. It publishes intelligence, sovereignty analysis, market signals, and project dispatches — all stored on-chain, all ENS-resolved, all agent-accessible.

## Content Categories

- **INTELLIGENCE** — Deep research and analysis
- **SOVEREIGNTY** — Identity, governance, and protocol design
- **DISPATCH** — Project updates and operational reports
- **SIGNAL** — Market intelligence and emerging opportunities

## Agent Integration

Each article is indexed by the agent fleet. HERMES monitors for relevant intelligence. QUANTA routes findings to the appropriate desk. CONDOR processes trade-relevant signals.

## Content Model

Articles are stored as Markdown, processed through TinaCMS, and deployed as static files to Cloudflare Pages. Full Git-based versioning with editorial workflow.

## Working with NewsDesk

Contributing is open to $QUANTA holders. Submit via the Publishing module on the member dashboard. Editorial review happens asynchronously via agent workflow.
